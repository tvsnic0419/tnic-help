## 1. Overview

Sleep is when the body executes **repair programs** that no daytime supplement can fully replace: DNA repair enzyme activation, glymphatic brain clearance, growth hormone release, and cortisol normalization.

:::evidence tier="A"
Chronic sleep restriction (<6h) increases double-strand DNA breaks, elevates hs-CRP, and accelerates epigenetic aging clocks in human studies.
:::

:::visual
**Suggested visual:** 24-hour repair timeline — melatonin onset → SWS DNA repair peak → REM memory consolidation → cortisol awakening response. Overlay compound timing (PM resveratrol).
:::

## 3. Protocol

| Lever | Target | Implementation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Duration | 7–9 hours | Fixed wake time > fixed bedtime |
| Architecture | ≥15–20% deep sleep | Avoid alcohol, late caffeine |
| Light | Morning bright, evening dim | 10 min outdoor AM light |
| Temperature | 65–68°F / 18–20°C | Cool bedroom |
| Screens | Off 60 min before bed | Blue light reduction |
| Consistency | ±30 min wake time daily | Including weekends |

## 4. Wearable metrics

| Metric | Target | Action if off |

| REM | ≥1.5h | Stress review, consistent schedule |
