## 1. Overview

Exercise is the **highest-impact lifestyle intervention** for longevity — no compound replicates the multi-hallmark signaling from consistent movement. It activates AMPK, PGC-1α, telomere maintenance pathways, and senescence clearance mechanisms.

:::evidence tier="A"
Meta-analyses link regular aerobic training with longer leukocyte telomeres, lower hs-CRP, and reduced all-cause mortality. Effect size exceeds most supplement interventions.
:::

:::visual
**Suggested visual:** Three-zone training pyramid — base: Zone 2 (70% volume), middle: resistance (2–3×/wk), peak: VO2max intervals (1×/wk). Hallmark icons at each tier.
:::

## 3. Weekly protocol template

| Type | Frequency | Duration | Intensity | Target |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Zone 2 cardio | 3–4×/week | 45–60 min | HR ~60–70% max | Mitochondrial base |
| Resistance | 2–3×/week | 30–45 min | 6–12 reps, compound lifts | Muscle + glucose disposal |
| VO2max intervals | 1×/week | 20–30 min | 4×4 min @ 85–95% max HR | Cardiorespiratory ceiling |
| Mobility / recovery | Daily | 10–15 min | Low | Injury prevention |

**Minimum effective dose:** 150 min moderate aerobic + 2 resistance sessions per week (WHO baseline).

## 4. Biomarker proxies

| Metric | Tool | Target trend | Hallmark proxy |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Resting heart rate | Wearable | ↓ 2–5 bpm over 3 months | Mitochondrial efficiency |

| hs-CRP | Lab | <1.0 mg/L | Inflammation |
| Fasting glucose | Lab | <100 mg/dL | Nutrient sensing |
| VO2max | Test or estimate | ↑ or stable with age | All-cause mortality predictor |
