## What happens

**Inflammaging** is chronic, low-grade, systemic inflammation that increases with age without acute infection. Drivers include:

- Senescent cell SASP secretion
- Gut barrier dysfunction (endotoxin leakage)
- Visceral adipose tissue IL-6 production
- Immunosenescence and inflammasome activation

Clinically, **hs-CRP** is the most accessible biomarker. Values below 1.0 mg/L are optimal; above 3.0 signals elevated cardiovascular risk.

## The NRF2 counterbalance

NRF2 is not just an antioxidant switch — it directly suppresses NF-κB, the master inflammatory transcription factor. This is why the TNiC NRF2 stack (sulforaphane + GlyNAC) targets inflammaging at the signaling level, not just symptom management.
