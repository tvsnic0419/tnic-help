> **TL;DR** — Chaperones and proteasomes fall behind misfolded protein load. Glutathione protects cysteine residues critical for folding. GlyNAC is the lead OTC intervention; heat shock via exercise is the free layer. Track energy, recovery, and GSH.

## What this hallmark means

**Proteostasis** is the network of chaperones, the ubiquitin-proteasome system, and autophagy that keeps proteins folded and cleared. With age, misfolded aggregates accumulate — amyloid, lipofuscin, and damaged enzymes — because redox and clearance capacity decline together.

| Failure mode | What changes | What you feel |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Redox folding errors** | Oxidized cysteines misfold | Afternoon brain fog, exercise intolerance |
| **Proteasome slowdown** | Tagged proteins not cleared | Slow recovery, "heavy" limbs |
| **Chaperone decline** | HSP70/90 response blunted | Heat/cold stress feels harder |

A **hub hallmark** — links to autophagy, mitochondrial dysfunction, and inflammation when aggregates trigger innate immune sensors.

## Why this matters for your protocol

Glutathione is the **master redox buffer for cysteine residues** in folding proteins. That is why GlyNAC — not generic vitamin C — anchors TNiC proteostasis protocols. Sulforaphane adds NRF2-driven proteasome subunit upregulation.

**Intervention hierarchy (evidence-weighted):**

1. **Sleep + fasting window** — autophagy and chaperone cycles need rest periods
2. **GlyNAC** — restores GSH pool (Tier A human data)
3. **Sulforaphane** — NRF2 → proteasome gene induction
4. **Exercise heat shock** — HSP response (Tier A)

:::evidence tier="A"
Kumar GlyNAC RCTs demonstrate GSH restoration and improved mitochondrial/proteostatic markers (PMID 34129059). Sulforaphane induces NRF2 target genes including proteasome components (PMID 18454171).
:::

## Red flags — when to pause or escalate

:::redflag title="Stop and consult before intensifying"
- **Rapid cognitive decline** — proteostasis supplements do not treat neurodegeneration
- **Liver enzymes rising on TUDCA or high-dose NAC** — discontinue and repeat LFTs
- **Eating disorder history** — fasting/autophagy protocols require clinical support
:::
