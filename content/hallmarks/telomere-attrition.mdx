## Overview

Telomeres are protective caps on chromosome ends. Each division shortens them until cells enter senescence. Stress, inflammation, and oxidative damage accelerate attrition beyond replication alone.

## TNiC focus

Lower compound coverage (45%) — lifestyle interventions (stress reduction, exercise) rank highest by evidence. NMN provides indirect support via sirtuin pathways.
