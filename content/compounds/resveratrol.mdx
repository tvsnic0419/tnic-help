## 1. Overview

Trans-resveratrol activates **SIRT1** and **AMPK**, mimicking caloric restriction signaling. Micronized formulations achieve 3–5× higher plasma levels. Pairs synergistically with NAD+ precursors.

**Hallmarks:** Mitochondrial dysfunction · Epigenetic alterations · Chronic inflammation · Cellular senescence

:::evidence tier="B"
Strong mechanistic and preclinical data. Human trial outcomes mixed for standalone resveratrol; synergy with NMN has stronger rationale (cofactor + activator). PMID 30930169.
:::

:::visual
**Suggested visual:** SIRT1 deacetylation cycle — NAD+ consumption → chromatin remodeling → PGC-1α → mitochondrial biogenesis. Highlight PM timing with fat-containing meal for absorption.
