> **TL;DR** — Spermidine is a dietary polyamine that induces autophagy via EP300 inhibition. Tier B: Madeo 2021 *Cell* RCT improved memory in older adults; epidemiology links intake to longevity. 1–6 mg supplemental or wheat-germ dietary sources; retest cognition and fasting markers at week 12.

## 1. What spermidine does (and why it matters)

Autophagy — cellular self-recycling — declines with age. Spermidine reactivates cleanup pathways without requiring extended fasting, making it a mechanistic complement to mTOR-modulating lifestyle and NAD+ restoration.

**Primary hallmarks targeted:** Disabled autophagy · Epigenetic alterations · Cellular senescence

:::evidence tier="B"
PMID 33932338 — Spermidine supplementation improves memory performance and mitochondrial function in older adults (*Cell Reports Medicine* / Madeo group, 2021). PMID 30093609 — mechanistic autophagy review (*Science*).
:::

## 2. Mechanism

| Pathway | Mechanism | Hallmark link |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EP300 inhibition | Hypusinates eIF5A → autophagy gene program | Autophagy |
| Histone modulation | Alters acetylation landscape | Epigenetic alterations |
| SASP modulation | Preclinical reduction of senescence secretome | Senescence |

:::practical title="When spermidine is worth your money"
Spermidine earns a stack slot when **two or more** apply:

- Autophagy or proteostasis is a top hallmark priority
- You already practice time-restricted eating and want pharmacological support
- Memory/cognitive maintenance is a stated goal

**Skip or defer** if you are on immunosuppressants without physician review.
:::

## 3. Evidence summary

| Study | Design | Key outcomes | Tier |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Madeo 2021 | Human RCT | Memory improvement, mitochondrial markers | B |
| Epidemiology | Cohort | Dietary spermidine inversely associated with mortality | B |

**Consensus:** Best human data is cognitive/metabolic pilot outcomes — not lifespan endpoints. Tier B autophagy adjunct.

## 4. Dosing protocol

- **Supplement:** 1–6 mg spermidine-rich extract daily
- **Dietary:** Wheat germ, aged cheese, legumes (variable content)
- **Pair with:** PM resveratrol or fasting windows for autophagy synergy
- **Retest:** Cognitive self-assessment + optional fasting glucose at week 12
