## 1. Overview

Longevity protocols without measurement are guesswork. This guide maps **what to test, when to retest, and how to interpret trends** — tied to TNiC biomarkers, hallmark risks, and stack-adjustment triggers.

:::visual
**Suggested visual:** Timeline roadmap — Month 0 (baseline panel) → Month 3 (check-in) → Month 6 (adjustment) → Month 12 (full retest). Color-coded by Tier 1 vs Tier 2 markers.
:::

## 5. Interpretation playbook

### Status tiers (TNiC Labs)

| Status | Meaning | Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Optimal | In target range | Maintain protocol |
| Watch | Borderline | Review compliance, lifestyle, dose |
| Critical | Significantly off | Physician consult; do not escalate supplements blindly |

### Trend rules

| Direction | Good for | Bad for |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ↑ | GSH, NAD+, AKG | — |
| ↓ | hs-CRP, oxLDL, 8-OHdG | — |
| ↑ | — | hs-CRP, oxLDL, 8-OHdG |
| ↓ | — | GSH, NAD+ |

**Rule:** Single point-in-time values matter less than **direction over 2+ timepoints**.

:::evidence tier="A"
Biomarker-guided protocol adjustment is standard practice in clinical trials. TNiC applies the same logic to personal longevity tracking.
:::

## 6. Stack-adjustment triggers

| Observation | Likely cause | Adjustment |
| --- | --- | --- |
| GSH low after 24 wk GlyNAC | Compliance, glycine insufficiency | Verify dose; add dietary glycine |
| NAD+ low after 4 wk NMN | Product quality, dose | Verify third-party tested NMN; increase to 500 mg |
| hs-CRP rising | Stress, sleep, infection | Lifestyle first; do not add antioxidants for acute infection |
| oxLDL rising | Diet, not stack | Review fat quality; maintain NRF2 triad |
| All markers optimal | Protocol working | Maintain 6-month monitoring |
